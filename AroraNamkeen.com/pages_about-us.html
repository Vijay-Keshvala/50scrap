PRIDE OF INDORE PHILOSOPHY

Pride of Indore philosophy is all about delivering the best and the tastiest namkeens. The taste of Indore is its pride and we at Arora Namkeen Udyog are committed to this philosophy.

Trust, Innovation and creativity are the very core of Arora Namkeen Udyog values. All our products are a result of these values and we ensure each product is delicious and is a class apart. Right from the sourcing to manufacturing processes these values are an integral part of the chain.

The humble beginnings of Arora Namkeen go back to 1940 when a hard-working visionary started producing namkeens in Indore. Over the last seven decades, there has been a strong and symbiotic relationship between the Indori Namkeen and Arora Namkeen. The company has grown with time from a small cottage industry to being almost synonymous with word Indori Namkeen across the globe. We as one of the oldest namkeens producer from Indore believe that it is our privelege to bring the taste of Indore to everyone.
