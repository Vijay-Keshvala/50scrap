    • About Us
    Balvvardhak | Healthy Baby Food Brand in India

    Mission Statement

    To nourish families with wholesome, healthy food for indian babies and kids worldwide, honoring traditional Indian food culture and thus promoting healthy living. We aim to support parents in nurturing their children with nutritious options, while enhancing our capabilities to deliver only fresh products, generate employment, and promote financial literacy. We also strive to educate parents on the benefits of Indian superfoods and holistic child-rearing practices

    Vision Statement

    “To be a leading provider of trusted, high-quality foods rooted in traditional Indian superfoods that bring the taste of indian home to every meal, fostering a healthier and happier community.”

    Founder and Co- Founders

    Name: Anvay Sallunke

    Role: Founder

    Anvay was the first consumer of Balvvardhak baby foods when I didn’t even think of it this way, back then. Only motto of mine as a mom was that I didn’t want to feed my child processed, sugary and adulterated baby food from the market. So the journey began with Anvay and as he tasted cheerfully, rejected quite a many trials, settled on few, gulped and clapped during our quality checks of my many baby food versions, he was the ultimate supervisor. A very strict, honest but playful supervisor I had, I must say!

    Name: Amruta Sallunke
    Role: Co-Founder and Owner

    Amruta, a biotechnologist by qualification, brings her expertise and love for cooking to Balvvardhak Foods. Amruta’s journey began with a simple desire to provide tahe best nutrition for her own child. Her dedication to using only Indian-origin grains and pulses led to the creation of Balvvardhak Foods, ensuring every baby gets the nutrition they deserve.” She ensures that every product is made with the utmost care and nutritional value.

    Name: Bhusaan Sallunke
    Role: Co-Founder, Consultant and advisor
    Bhusaan Sallunke, the driving force behind Balvvardhak Foods, brings a wealth of experience from his previous role as a Marketing Sales Manager at an esteemed organization.
    His expertise in market analysis, strategic planning, and customer engagement has been instrumental in establishing Balvvardhak Foods as a trusted name.
