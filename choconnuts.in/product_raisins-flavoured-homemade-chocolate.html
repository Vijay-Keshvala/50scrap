Raisins Flavoured Homemade Chocolate

₹350.00 – ₹1,400.00

Raisins have this lovely chewy property that transforms into utter indulgence when paired with chocolate. They taste a little sweet, a little sour, very tart and sometimes they are juicy – it is these properties that make them a great pair with the headiness of chocolate. Try Choco-N-Nuts’ homemade raisins chocolate for a taste that is on the middle ground, everywhere. They’re just enough sour, just enough chocolaty, just enough sweet and majorly tasty!

We urge you to try this homemade chocolate at least once, even if you are not a fan of raisins; you will surely come back for more! Available in dark, milk and white chocolate versions.
