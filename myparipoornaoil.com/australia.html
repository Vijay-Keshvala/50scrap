Wooden Cold-Pressed Oils Exporter From India To Australia

Paripoorna Oil is a renowned brand in exporting cold-pressed, nutrient-rich oils to Australia. Our premium oils are made using ancient wooden chekku (cold press) methods, where the natural fragrance, flavor, and therapeutic properties are preserved. We are FSSAI-certified, ISO 9001:2015-certified, and AIAO-BAR-accredited, following international purity, safety, and authenticity standards. Our product range includes Cold-Pressed Coconut Oil, Groundnut Oil, and Sesame Oil, all suitable for cooking, skincare, and general care.

To guarantee the best quality, Paripoorna Oil directly procures raw materials from farmers associated with our Farmer Producer Organization (FPO). We have forged healthy relationships with these farmers for more than ten years, ensuring pesticide-free cultivation. This valued relationship allows us to produce oils that are pure, traceable, and ethically sourced. We produce only pesticide-free cold-pressed oils using pesticide-free ingredients. With our private labeling and export assistance, Paripoorna provides the best of nature to homes and businesses all over Australia.
