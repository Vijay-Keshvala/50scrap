Wooden/Cold-Pressed Sesame Seed Oil Extraction Method

Step 01: Seed Preparation

We thoroughly wash the sesame seeds to remove pebbles, dust, and other substances we do not need. We dry them in the sunlight after washing to preserve flavor and freshness.

01

Step 02: Wood Pressing

We crush the seeds manually in a wooden press traditional in nature (Marachekku) so that the process is chemical-free. Natural palm jaggery mixes with the seeds to make the oil full of aroma and a balanced taste.

02

Step 03: Oil Extraction

The sesame oil flows out slowly during pressing, keeping all its natural nutrients intact. We avoid heat or artificial methods to protect the oilâ€™s purity.

03

Step 04: Settling Process

We store the fresh oil in clean settling tanks to let any fine particles drop to the bottom. This resting stage makes the oil clearer and richer in flavor.

04

Step 05: Filtering & Packaging

We gently filter the oil to preserve its natural aroma and nutrition. We then package it fresh, unpreserved, in our customer-specific containers.
