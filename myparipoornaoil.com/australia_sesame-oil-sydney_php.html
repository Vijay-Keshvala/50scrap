Traditional Pure Cold-Pressed Sesame Oil Making Process

Step 01: Preparation of Seeds Process

We begin by cleaning to eliminate dust and particles from sesame or gingelly seeds. After that, we dry them in the sun to keep the flavor and improve the quality of our cold-pressed sesame oil.

01

Step 02: Wood Pressing Method

We crush the dried seeds together with palm jaggery in a traditional Marachekku (wood press) for natural balance. This method keeps nutrients and creates the smell of sesame or gingelly oil.

02

Step 03: Extraction of Oil

When we squeeze the seeds, pure sesame oil begins to flow, full of natural proteins and healthy fats. This procedure holds the nutrients in the oil, without heat or chemicals.

03

Step 04: Settling Phase Process

We leave the extracted sesame oil in clean tanks to settle for four days. This process separates impurities naturally, keeping the oil clean and uncontaminated.

04

Step 05: Filtering or Packing Process

We filter the oil to extract the sediments without destroying its rich and natural quality. We ensure to pack 100% pure sesame oil without using any preservatives or refining.
