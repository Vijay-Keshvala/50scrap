Know More About the Sesame Oil

Sesame, a flowering plant native to the East Indies, is one of the oldest crops used to produce oil, with seeds containing up to 60% oil. It contains vitamins and minerals such as copper, manganese, calcium, magnesium, phosphorus, zinc, selenium, E, and B1.
