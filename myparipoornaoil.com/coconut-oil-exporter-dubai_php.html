Cold-Pressed Coconut Oil Manufacturing & Export Process

Step 01: Selection of the Best Coconuts

We buy fresh and mature coconuts from organic farms that we trust. We very closely inspect every coconut to ensure that the coconut meets our high standards. This helps us to make pure coconut oil, including the full benefit of nature.

01

Step 02: Using Traditional Wooden Presses

We press the oil using wooden machines that operate under low temperatures slowly. The method preserves the natural odor, taste, and nutrients of the coconut oil. There are no chemicals or heat used in any part of the process.

Step 03: Natural Filtration and Testing

After pressing , oil settles down by itself naturally. We then filter it carefully. We check all batches for moisture, color, and purity. Our local and international quality regulations ensure that the oil is clean and safe.
