Organic Wooden Cold-Pressed Oils Exporter in the United States

Paripoorna is a reliable exporter of high-quality cold-pressed oils, providing fresh, unprocessed products to the U.S. market. Our oils are produced using traditional wooden cold-press techniques, which help retain their original nutrients, essence, and taste. We are certified by FSSAI and accredited by AIAO-BAR, adhering to international standards of food safety and purity. Our products include Cold-Pressed Coconut Oil, Peanut Oil, and Sesame Oil, which are best suited for cooking, skincare, and holistic well-being.

To ensure the highest quality, Paripoorna Oil procures raw materials directly from farmers affiliated with our Farmer Producer Organization (FPO). We have maintained strong relationships with these farmers for over a decade, guaranteeing pesticide-free farming practices. This long-term partnership allows us to produce oils that are traceable, clean, and ethically made. Using only pesticide-free raw materials, we manufacture premium cold-pressed oils. With our private labeling and export support, Paripoorna brings the best of nature to homes and businesses across the U.S.
