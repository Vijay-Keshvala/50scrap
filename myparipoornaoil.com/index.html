How We Work?

Traditional Wood Pressing Method for Oil Extraction

Seed Preparation

Fresh seeds (such as groundnut, coconut, sesame, or mustard) are thoroughly cleaned to remove impurities and then sun-dried to ensure moisture content is low, which is important for efficient oil extraction.

Wood Pressing

The dried seeds are placed in a traditional wooden press , where they are crushed and pressed. This process uses slow mechanical pressure to extract the oil without generating heat, thereby retaining the natural flavours, nutrients, and antioxidants.

Oil Filtration

After extraction, the raw oil is allowed to settle for a few days, during which any solid particles or sediments settle at the bottom. The clear, pure oil is then filtered without using any chemicals.
