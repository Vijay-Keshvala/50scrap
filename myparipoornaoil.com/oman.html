Wooden Cold-Pressed Oils Exporter From India To Oman

Paripoorna Oil is one of the top exporters of pure cold-pressed oils in Oman made by the ancient wooden chekku (cold-press) process. This traditional method honors nature’s way, ensuring the original aroma, taste, and medicinal properties are retained in every single drop. We abide by the international standards of quality, purity, and safety with the agreement of FSSAI, ISO 9001:2015, and AIAO-BAR.

Our brand consists of Cold-Pressed Coconut Oil, Groundnut Oil, Mustard Oil, and Sesame Oil, and these are the four products we export to Oman, where they are used both in the kitchen, and as a beauty and wellness product. Besides, we have a very close relationship with our Farmer Producer Organization (FPO) from which we get raw materials directly from farmers who have been growing pesticide-free seeds for more than ten years. By this intimate partnership, we offer you oils that are premium quality, environmentally friendly, and 100% origin guaranteed.

By means of private labeling and export support, Paripoorna Oil is well-equipped to cater to the demand of the Oman market for top of the line, chemical-free, cold-pressed oil which is for the use of households, health stores, and business partners.
