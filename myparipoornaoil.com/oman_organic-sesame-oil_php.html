Wood-Pressed Gingelly Oil Manufacturing & Exporting Process

Step 01: Sesame Seed Preparation

We clean sesame (gingelly) seeds to remove dust, stones, and other bits. Once cleaned, the seeds are sun-dried. This helps lock in their natural flavor and supports better oil quality.

01

Step 02: Wooden Pressing Method

The dried seeds are pressed in a wooden Marachekku with a little palm jaggery. This slow pressing keeps the nutrients safe and brings out the strong, nutty smell of sesame.

02

Step 03: Natural Oil Extraction

The oil comes out slowly from the seeds and stays pure, without chemicals. It keeps the natural fats, proteins, and nutrients inside.

03

Step 04: Settling Phase

The oil is kept in clean tanks for four days. During this time, the sediments settle naturally. This makes the oil clear and ready for filtering, without losing its natural quality.

04

Step 05: Filtering and Packing

The oil is lightly filtered to remove the sediments but keeps its natural richness. We pack it pure, without preservatives, in different sizes for bulk orders. In Oman, it is available in PET bottles, glass jars, or tin containers. We can also make custom packs to match your brand look.
