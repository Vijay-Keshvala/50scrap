Private Label Wooden Cold Pressed Oils Exporter in Qatar

Qatar’s demand for premium cold pressed oils is growing as people increasingly prefer clean food, natural wellness products, and trusted global sources. Consumers choose healthy cooking oils that fit modern lifestyles while honoring traditional food values. Paripoorna Oil meets this demand by exporting pure cold pressed oils to Qatar using the ancient wooden chekku process. This traditional method preserves the natural aroma, original taste, and wellness benefits in every drop. We strictly follow international quality and safety standards and comply with FSSAI, ISO 9001 2015, and AIAO BAR certifications.

Our product range includes cold pressed Coconut Oil, Groundnut Oil, Mustard Oil, and Sesame Oil. These oils serve both kitchen needs and beauty and wellness routines across Qatar. Each product reflects our commitment to natural processing and chemical free production.

We source our raw materials directly from a trusted Farmer Producer Organization. The farmers have cultivated pesticide free seeds for over ten years. This close partnership ensures premium quality, environmental responsibility, and complete origin assurance.

With strong private labeling capabilities and reliable export support, Paripoorna Oil is well prepared to supply Qatar with high quality cold pressed oils. We support households, health stores, and business partners who seek pure, natural, and dependable oil solutions.
