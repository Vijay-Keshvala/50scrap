Queries on Using Chekku Wood Pressed Oil in Daily Life

The market's growth is primarily driven by high demand for cold pressed oils from a variety of applications, including personal care, food & beverages, and animal feeds. Furthermore, excellent nutritional value, as well as better flavor, are important factors driving market growth.

When refined oils entail chemical extraction and heating, they can last up to two years longer than cold-pressed oils, which have a shelf life of six months to a year. If you want your cold-pressed oils to last as long as possible, keep them out of direct sunlight and store them in dark containers.

Reusing oil should be avoided because it tends to go rancid and produces more trans-fatty acids, which are dangerous free radicals for your health. Cold-pressed oils should not be heated again due to their lower smoking point.

The oil's safety depends on the storage duration before bottling and the type of filtering used.
