About
Star
Impact
Pvt
Ltd

Over the past decades the athletics and sporting goods manufacturing industry has coached itself towards development and success. Star Impact Pvt. Ltd. was established in 1997 by our Honorable Managing Director Mr. Mohammad Owais in Malerkotla (Pb) with registered brands “SEGA” and “QUIVE”.
Star Impact Pvt. Ltd. is one of the leading manufacturer and exporter of athletics and sporting shoes and accessories for a wide variety of sports and fitness activities. SEGA is primarily engaged in design and development with expertise and tremendous deployment and growth in athletics and sporting goods manufacturing industry with an in depth knowledge and understanding.

Business Description: Star Impact Pvt. Ltd. manufactures and sells footwear for Adults and kids for specific athletic use such as Football, Cricket, Badminton, Wrestling, Kabaddi, Hockey, Track racing, Running and Jogging. Apart from this SEGA manufactures Footballs, Shin Pads, Football Stockings, Kit bags, Gym Bags etc.

Apart from Sports segment, SEGA has launched its world class Genuine Leather formal shoes for Occasional, Formal and Professional purposes. We are serving Indian Army by manufacturing and supplying Army Shoes with high quality genuine leather upper and Rubber sole for more comfort and durability.

Infrastructure: At present Company is running its 02 manufacturing Plants based on approx. 1,17,000 and 5,44,200 sq./ft. area fully equipped with latest machinery and upgraded technologies. We have our dedicated Research and Development Dept. with latest reverse engineering tools and devices.

Customer Satisfaction: We have achieved satisfaction of over 3000 valuable customers by catering them the products with perfect design, high Quality and Durability. Each component of our products has been manufacture with latest techniques and upgraded technologies to give super grip and comfort for best Performance.

E-Commerce Platforms: In order to keep our valuable customers real-time updates of new product launches, exclusive deals and one touch Purchase, we are available on E-commerce platforms like Udaan, Ajio, Amazon and Flip cart and on Company dedicated websites as follows:
For India: - www.segashoes.com and www.starimpact.co.in

International Business: SEGA and QUIVE are registered in Birmingham (UK) as “SEGA INTERNATIONAL” & “QUIVE LONDON” We have SEGA exclusive showroom as well as Warehouse to keep ready stock in order to fulfill demands of the customers.
