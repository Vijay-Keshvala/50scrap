About The Founders

Aanan Khurma

Co-Founder & CEO

Aanan Khurma is a passionate advocate for healthspan maximise, aiming to help humans live up to 120 years disease-free. His journey in healthcare began in 2008 with research on lab-on-a-chip devices for blood analysis at Massey University, New Zealand. In 2010, he joined IIT Delhi as a Project Scientist to establish and lead the Medical Devices & Research Lab. Aanan is an alumnus of Stanford Biodesign and has experience launching a medtech startup, Hansure, which was backed by Microsoft Ventures, Johnson & John-son, and BD.

Aditya Seth

Co-Founder, Brands & Products

Aditya Seth brings over a decade of experience in manufacturing operations
and supply chain management, with a specialized focus on nutrition and
functional food products. His expertise lies in identifying market gaps and
leading technical research and branding teams to develop products with
strong product-market fits. Having been trained by experts from NestlÃ© and
Unilever, Aditya has mastered the complexities of supply chain operations in

Ripunjay Chachan

Co-Founder, Channels & Distribution

Ripunjay Chachan, Co-founder of Wellversed and a Stanford Biodesign alumnus, is a lifelong builder at the intersection of health, behavior, and distribution. With over a decade of experience decoding consumer patterns and scaling data-driven systems, he has worked with leading eCommerce brands to reimagine how health products reach people. Guided by a vision to democratize wellness at scale, he aims to impact a billion lives by making long-term fitness and wellbeing a natural part of everyday living
