About Us

Our journey started in 1975 when Mr. Jagjivandas Govindji Patel dreamed of being part of everyone’s morning glass of milk and their cherished celebrations. Every dairy product, every bite of sweet, and every moment of indulgence is a promise fulfilled of taste, trust, and timeless tradition. For us, it is about being part of the ‘Celebration ka Swad’ moment.
